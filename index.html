Satellite data allow analysis of environmental indicators such as air quality, temperature, and vegetation cover. These data are integrated into interactive maps to help urban planners make sustainable decisions.

The map data are simulations inspired by real observations from NASA satellites such as MODIS and VIIRS, used to monitor air quality and surface temperature.

It helps detect areas vulnerable to pollution or extreme heat, assisting in designing green infrastructure strategies, sustainable mobility, and improving citizen well-being.
